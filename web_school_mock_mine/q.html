106 英語分級測驗 keyboard_arrow_right 考前說明
The Listening section measures your ability to understand conversations and lectures in English. You will hear each conversation and lecture only one time. After each conversation or lecture, you will hear some questions about it. Answer all questions based on what the speakers state or imply. You may take notes while you listen. You may use your notes to help you answer the questions.Most questions have four possible answers. In some questions, you will see this icon: . This means that you will hear, but not see, part of the question.
