第二部份朗讀句子與短文

共有五個句子及一篇短文。請先用1分鐘的時間閱讀試卷上的句子與短文，然後在1分鐘內以正常的速度清楚正確的朗讀一遍。

One: Did you read today’s newspaper?
Two: Was Mary second in the race?
Three: What did you say to the math teacher?
Four: Doing exercise is important.
Five: To see is to believe.
Six: Josie and Lily are sisters and spend a lot of time online after school every day. But they have only one computer, so they have agreed to take turns. On weeknights, Josie uses it after dinner, between 7 and 8:30, while Lily uses it from 8:30 to 10, before she goes to bed. They never need to fight for the computer.
